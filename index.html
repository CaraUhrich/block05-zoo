Animal Identifying Reference

Bears

  • Ollie

    Ollie is male and slightly larger with more brown on his ears. He prefers to be little spoon and hates nuts.

  • Mona

    Mona is female and has rounder ears. She loves curling up in a ball and stealing salmon.

Giraffes

  • Frankie

    Frankie is slightly shorter and has an asymentrical gait. She will investigate you as soon as you get close enough.

  • Coconut

    Coco is slightly taller and has more markings on his face. He will hide unless you have food.

Lions

  • Mella

    Mella is a female lion. She has some rbf but loves head scritches.

  • Karl

    Karl is a very sweet deaf old man.

Monkeys

  • Cookie

    Cookie has kind eyes and is missing a toe on her left foot. She enjoys preening and watching you from afar.

  • Earl

    Earl has a long face and receding hairline. He loves puzzles and being quietly judgmental.

  • Bananna Pudding

    Banana Pudding is a chaos goblin with a round face. He is always playing with his ball and investigating everything with his hands.

Alligators

  • Wren

    Wren is our smallest and sassiest alligator. Don't get too close.

  • Aspen

    Aspen is the medium sized one. She is a cuddle bug but doesn't like the smell of perfume.

  • Mika

    Mika is a big boy and knows it. You can get close, but don't try to touch him.
